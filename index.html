The Problem

A ₹3.6 Lakh Crore Market
Running on Trust & WhatsApp

India's rental market operates on informal norms, unverifiable claims, and unequal power dynamics. No scalable, neutral, digital platform exists.

01

Trust Deficit

No portable record of tenant or landlord behaviour. Both parties routinely lie during negotiations with zero accountability.

02

Deposit Disputes

Unfair deposit deductions at move-out with no documented evidence. No neutral system to evaluate property condition changes.

03

Broker Opacity

Commission structures aren't documented. Brokers vanish after the deal. No tracking, ratings, or incentive to perform post-closure.

04

Paper Agreements

Informal rental agreements are legally weak, easily lost, and impossible to enforce — leaving both parties vulnerable.

05

Maintenance Chaos

No formal issue-tracking. Disputes about responsibilities fester without documented evidence or SLAs.

06

NRI Landlord Gap

Remote & NRI landlords have zero visibility into their property. They depend on unreliable middlemen for everything.
