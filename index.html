What is Nivantro?

Nivantro is India's first neutral, digital rental platform that protects landlords, tenants, and brokers equally. Think of it as a trusted middleman that replaces the chaos of WhatsApp groups, verbal promises, and paper agreements with a transparent, technology-driven system.

What problem does it solve?

India's ₹3.6 Lakh Crore rental market runs entirely on informal trust. Landlords can't verify tenants. Tenants get unfair deposit cuts. Brokers vanish after deals. Nivantro brings digital trust scores, photo-documented inspections, escrow-protected deposits, and milestone-based broker commissions — so every party is protected.
