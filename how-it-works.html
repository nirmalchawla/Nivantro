Product Roadmap

Phased Rollout Plan

Building the platform in four strategic phases, starting with the trust foundation.

Phase 1 — Foundation (Months 1–4)

Trust Ecosystem & Digital Agreements

User onboarding, property listing, Trust Score & RCP system, digital rental agreements with Aadhaar eSign, and basic payment tracking.

Phase 2 — Operations (Months 5–8)

Checklist, Ticketing & Deposit Escrow

Move-in/Move-out digital checklists, issue ticketing with SLAs, deposit escrow management, and broker commission model.

Phase 3 — Protection (Months 9–12)

Insurance Products & Arbitration

Deposit Shield (tenant insurance), RentGuard (landlord insurance), arbitration module, and legal support expansion.

Phase 4 — Scale (Months 13–18)

Mobile App & Market Expansion

React Native mobile apps (iOS + Android), Tier-2 city expansion, vernacular language support, and advanced analytics.
