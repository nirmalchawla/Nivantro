Module 01

Trust & Rating System

Every user on the platform carries a Trust Score between 1.0 and 5.0, publicly visible on their profile. The score is a weighted composite of behavioural signals accumulated across every deal. It cannot be deleted, hidden, or reset.

Good behaviour is rewarded; bad behaviour is made visible. This creates a self-correcting ecosystem where the incentive to act honestly is built into the product.

Module 03

Tenant Credit Points — Rental CIBIL

India's credit infrastructure scores borrowers. There is no equivalent for rental behaviour. A tenant's rental payment history is one of the most reliable indicators of financial responsibility — and today it is completely invisible. RCP changes that.

Score Bands:

600–749 Good Trusted badge, fast-track matching
400–599 Fair Standard access
